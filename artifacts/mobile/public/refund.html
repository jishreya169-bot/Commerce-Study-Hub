Refund Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

1. General Refund Rules

We want you to be satisfied with our courses and services. If you are not completely satisfied, we offer a refund within 7 days of the original purchase date, subject to the conditions below.

2. Eligibility for Refund

  • The refund request must be submitted within 7 days of the purchase.
  • You must have completed less than 20% of the course content.
  • Digital downloads and specific one-on-one sessions are strictly non-refundable.

3. Process for Requesting a Refund

To request a refund, please contact our support team at support@webvibezzz.com with your receipt or proof of purchase.

4. Processing Time

Once your return is received and inspected, we will send you an email to notify you that we have received your request. Approved refunds will be processed, and a credit will automatically be applied to your credit card or original method of payment, within a certain amount of days.
